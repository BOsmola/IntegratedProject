The presentation should be 2-4 PowerPoint slides based on the information in the planning report. The lecturer also informed us that we will need to make sure our project uses original data that we collect ourselves and not to use data collected by anyone else. The lecturer informed us that Glasgow city centre has air quality machines, and they post their data online. This will allow us to compare our data to theirs and even use that data to properly calibrate our device. The lecturer also mentioned that union street would be the best location for the project as he did something similar and got good data from union street.
